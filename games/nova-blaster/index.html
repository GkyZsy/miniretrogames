Nova Blaster

A Mega Man-inspired 2D action platformer built with vanilla JavaScript.

PauseP

Goal

Defeat drones, collect energy cores, and reach the gate in each stage.

Tips

  • Blaster shots can destroy patrol drones.
  • Dash to cross longer gaps safely.
  • Collect blue cores to heal.

Stage Flow

Three compact levels, boss fight in the final arena, best score saved locally.
